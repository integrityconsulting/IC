Who We Are

We are Integrity Consulting. We aim to provide the Muslim population in Canada with a fair chance of participating in sustainable money growth and tax-deferrement through RRSP RESP and TFSA without this having to contradict religious sentiments in the process.

How Does it Work?

Setting up a Shari' Compliant RESP, RRSP, TFSA etc. is challenging. Our service is to guide you step by step how to set one up. We then provide you with a diverse portfolio of 20 shares that are Shari' Compliant according to the international standards laid out by AAOIFI and in which you can invest according to your personal discretion. In addition, we also provide the service of calculating the amount of Zakat that needs to be paid on a per share basis.

Target Audience

Our service is specifically catered to reach out to the common Muslim person who wants to see his money grow, save for the education of his children but yet, due to religious sentiments and a lack of grounding in finance is deprived of the opportunity to participate in these investment vehicles. They are God-fearing people who work normal jobs and have better things to do than delving into the intricacies of AAOIFI-standards and how this applies to the stock market, and more important, how to calculate Zakat on them.
